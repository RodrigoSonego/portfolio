Dicest Dungeon

Turn-based game for GMTK Jam 2022

Summary

Dicest Dungeon is a game heavily inspired by the likes of Darkest Dungeon, where you walk through a dungeon (or, in this case, a casino) and fight monsters in turn-based battles. The game has you walking through a casino in order to take down the boss. In this casino, the battles are a bet, and your only weapon is your dice (which you literally throw at your enemy)!

Responsibilities

  • Game Design
  • Developing the turn-based combat
  • Developing the camera and character movement
  • Ensuring UI responsiveness
  • Bug fixing
